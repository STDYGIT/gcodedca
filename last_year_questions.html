README

Here, you can easily access coursework resources for each semester in Excel format. To begin, select your year from the dropdown menu. Once you've made your selection, you'll be able to view and download the resources available for that year and semester. Each card represents a specific semester and includes the latest updates and materials available for download. You can either click on the "Open Sheet" button to access the most recent versions of the resources or use the swipe gesture to download the PDF format of the sheet.
